How to take a screenshot in Windows 10?

In Windows 10 it is very easy to take a screenshot, just press Start + PrtSc and your screenshot will save on the "Pictures" folder . But if you want to take a screenshot of your current window or a portion of your window you need to use the Snipping tool. Press Start + Shift + S to snip your window.

There are four options:

  • Rectangular Snip
  • Freeform Snip
  • Window Snip
  • Full-screen Snip

Select one of the options and then click on the window you want to snip and

Save the screenshot.
