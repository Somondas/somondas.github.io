Personalize your Command Prompt



You can customize your command prompt to make it more like a hacking tool like shown in movies to look cool in front of your friends(just kidding). But it's practical, you can make your command prompt transparent so that you can write commands from your browser without minimizing any window.

To make your command prompt transparent, open the command prompt.

Right-click on the status bar.

Click on Properties.

Click on colors.

Lower your opacity and click OK.

You can also change the color of the text and background of your Command Prompt.
