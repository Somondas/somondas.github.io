What is touch typing?

Touch typing or blind typing is a style of typing where you type without using the sense of sight to find a particular key. a touch typist will know their location on the keyboard through muscle memory.

Tips for those who don't want to learn touch typing

If you don't want to learn touch typing, here are some tips that you can follow to increase your typing speed:

Use both hands for typing

Of course, you have to type using both hands. I see many of my friends who don't use both hands and they are slow as hell. So, try to use your both hands while typing.

Use your thumb to press the space bar

Use both thumbs to press the spacebar and you will notice that your typing speed is increasing.

Use keyboard shortcuts

Windows 10 has tones of keyboard shortcuts that you can memorize and minimize the use of the mouse.

Use your pinky fingers to press 'Shift', 'Enter', and 'Backspace'

Use your right pinky finger to press the 'Enter' key, 'Right Shift' key, and the 'Backspace' key. And use your left pinky finger to press the 'Left Shift', 'Left Ctrl, and the 'Capslock' key.

Use the 'Shift' key instead of 'Caps Lock'

Instead of using the Caps lock key, you can use the Shift key to capitalize a letter. Just press and hold the Shift key and press the letter you want to capitalize.
